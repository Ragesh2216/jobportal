About the Role

We're looking for a Senior Frontend Developer to join Google India's growing engineering team in Bangalore. You'll work on high-impact consumer products that reach billions of users globally. In this role, you'll own and develop complex UI systems, collaborate with cross-functional teams, and help shape the technical direction of the frontend stack.

Key Responsibilities
  • Build and maintain scalable, accessible, performant web applications using React and TypeScript
  • Collaborate with design, product, and backend teams to turn features from concept to production
  • Lead technical discussions, conduct code reviews, and mentor junior engineers
  • Optimise web performance across devices, browsers and network conditions
  • Contribute to architecture decisions and establish frontend best practices
  • Participate in on-call rotations and respond to production incidents
Requirements
  • 5+ years of professional frontend development experience
  • Proficiency in React, TypeScript, HTML5, CSS3, and modern build tools
  • Experience with state management libraries (Redux, Zustand, Jotai)
  • Solid understanding of web performance, accessibility (WCAG) and SEO
  • Strong communication skills and experience working in Agile teams
  • B.Tech/B.E. in Computer Science or equivalent practical experience
